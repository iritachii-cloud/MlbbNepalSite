Title: Kagayaki no Michi (輝きの道 / The Path of Radiance)

[Genre: Math Rock / Japanese Anime Opening / Epic Folk Rock]
[Tempo: 92 BPM]
[Key: E Minor]
[Vocal: Female, powerful and emotive with a wide range; clean, soaring vocals for chorus, breathy and intimate for verses]
[Mood: Epic, nostalgic, hopeful, triumphant, with moments of deep introspection]

[INTRO]

[The song begins with the sound of a distant, slow Large Drum (like a dhime) beating a steady, solemn rhythm, like a heartbeat. A solo Wooden Flute (like a bansuri) plays a melancholic, climbing melody over it, echoing across a vast landscape. The sound of a gentle breeze and the faint crackle of a distant fire. This fades as a Clean Electric Guitar plays a shimmering, arpeggiated chord progression, joined by a Finger-Picked Acoustic Guitar. The drums stop, leaving only the guitars and the sound of wind. A single, sustained note from the flute hangs in the air.]
[Instrumental Intro]

[VERSE 1]
[The Drums enter with a soft, intricate pattern on the hi-hat and toms. Bass Guitar joins, playing a melodic, walking line. The Flute weaves in and out, adding color. Electric Guitar plays clean, arpeggiated chords with a slight delay.]
[Sung softly, with a sense of wonder and memory]
遠い夜明けの 静けさの中で
風が運ぶ 古びた地図
指でなぞる 果てしない線路
鼓動だけが 道標
[The Flute echoes the final vocal melody line.]

[PRE-CHORUS]

[Electric Guitar shifts to a more driven, overdriven tone with a higher gain. Drums switch to a faster, more energetic beat on the ride cymbal and snare. Bass locks in with the kick drum. The Flute plays a soaring, rapid passage.]
[Voice grows stronger, more determined]

霞む空に 手を伸ばして
まだ見ぬ君の 声を探す
この一秒が 永遠なら
迷わずに 飛び込めるのに

[CHORUS]

[The full band explodes. Distorted Electric Guitars play powerful, open chords. The Flute plays a high, piercing counter-melody that cuts through the mix. Drums are powerful, with heavy kick and snare hits. Bass is driving and prominent.]
[Sung with full force, emotional peak]
輝く道よ この手を取って
砕けた夢が 星になる
心に灯る 不確かな火
君と出会う その日まで

音のない世界で 叫んでる
この想い 風に乗せて
繰り返す日々を 壊して
今、飛び立つよ

[POST-CHORUS]
[Music strips down to just the Acoustic Guitar and a simple Flute melody. The Large Drum returns with a single, booming hit on the downbeat of each measure. Soft Vocal Harmonies layer over the lead vocal.]
[Sung softly, with a breathy, intimate tone]
la la la...
風の彼方

la la la...
響く鼓動

[VERSE 2]

[The rhythm section returns with a more complex, math-rock inspired groove. Drums are syncopated with fills on the toms. Electric Guitar plays a clean, intricate riff with palm muting. Flute plays a playful, rhythmic melody that dances around the guitar.]

[Voice is more confident, with a hint of a smile]
石畳に 刻まれた傷
雨が洗う 古の約束
瞳閉じて 感じる景色
それはいつも 君の影
[The Flute plays a descending, melancholic run.]

[PRE-CHORUS 2]

[The Electric Guitar becomes more distorted, building tension. Drums use a fast, driving beat on the hi-hats. Bass plays a simple, powerful root note pattern.]
[Voice is more urgent, building to the chorus]
崩れそうな この世界で
繋いだ手は 離さないで
たとえ全て 壊れても
君だけは 守り抜く

[CHORUS]

[Repeat Chorus with even more intensity. Additional Orchestral Strings (sampled) swell in the background. Drums are more explosive. Vocal Harmonies are layered and powerful.]
[Full force, triumphant and defiant]
輝く道よ この手を取って
砕けた夢が 星になる
心に灯る 不確かな火
君と出会う その日まで

音のない世界で 叫んでる
この想い 風に乗せて
繰り返す日々を 壊して
今、飛び立つよ

[BRIDGE]

[The music drops to almost nothing. A single, sustained note from a Large Drum. A Wooden Flute plays a simple, ancient melody. The Acoustic Guitar plays sparse, open chords. The lead vocal is whispered, close-mic'd, full of emotion.]
[Whispered, intimate, like a prayer]
闇の中で 光るのは
君の声 君の温もり
この手に残る 微かな熱
それが 真実
[The Flute and Acoustic Guitar swell, building tension. The Large Drum begins to beat faster, like a racing heart.]

もう、迷わない

[FINAL CHORUS]

[The full band returns with maximum intensity. Distorted Guitars are massive. The Flute plays a triumphant, rapid solo. Drums are relentless. The Vocal is at its peak, with a powerful belt and harmonies. This is the climax of the song.]
[Epic, anthemic, full of hope and resolution]

輝く道よ この手を取って
砕けた夢が 星になる
心に灯る 不確かな火
君と出会う その日まで

この声よ 空を裂いて
終わらない夜を 照らして
運命さえ 追い風にして
今、飛び立つよ

[OUTRO]

[The band cuts out abruptly, leaving only the sound of a Wooden Flute playing the main melody, fading into the distance. A single, echoing Electric Guitar arpeggio. The sound of wind and distant rain. A final, soft beat on the Large Drum, like a fading heartbeat.]
[Music strips down, soft, fading into ambient noise]

La la la...
飛び立つ場所へ

La la la...
響く鼓動
[The flute note hangs in the air, then fades to silence. The faint crackle of a vinyl record.]

[Whispered, fading]
ずっと… 待っている
